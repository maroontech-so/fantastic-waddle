# 🎓 AdvocoDe — IT Club Community Hub & Messenger

Welcome to **AdvocoDe**, the ultimate digital ecosystem crafted specifically for IT Club members, computer science students, and tech enthusiasts. AdvocoDe is designed to bring students together, foster collaborative programming, host technical Q&As, share project accomplishments, and enable secure, real-time communication.

This guide is written for **students and members** of the IT Club to help you master the hub, earn Experience Points (XP), customize your profile, and connect with your peers.

---

## 🌟 Major Capabilities & Features

### 1. 📢 The Collaborative Forum Feed
Our central timeline is the heartbeat of the IT Club. This is where intellectual conversations happen, where errors are debugged, and where major project milestones are celebrated:
* **Rich Categories**: Filter posts by **Code Shares**, **Questions**, **Collaborations**, **Announcements**, or **Events**.
* **Interactive Code Blocks**: Code snippets are beautifully formatted with syntax highlighting. Copy code directly to your clipboard in a single click using the integrated **Copy Code** action.
* **Rich Reactions & Comments**: Give feedback with quick heart-likes, upvotes, downvotes, and detailed threaded comment sections.
* **Media Attachments**: Upload custom mockups, screenshots, or diagram attachments directly from your device to enrich your posts.

### 2. 💬 Real-Time AdvocoDe Messenger
A fully integrated, private chat system to communicate with your fellow club members, classmates, and project partners:
* **Instant Delivery & Sync**: Messages are transmitted in real time, powered by a fast cloud state sync.
* **Double-Tick Read Receipts**:
  * `✓` (Single Gray Tick): Sent successfully to the cloud.
  * `✓✓` (Double Gray Ticks): Delivered successfully to the recipient's device.
  * `✓✓` (Double Blue Ticks): Read by the recipient.
* **Tail-Pointer Chat Bubbles**: Elegant custom-designed speech bubbles with corner tails that point away from the center of the screen, mimicking professional messaging applications (WhatsApp/Telegram-style alignment).
* **Swipe Right to Reply (Mobile)**: Swipe any message bubble to the right to reply to it instantly with a linked context bubble!
* **Hold/Long-Press for Options (PC & Mobile)**: Left-click and hold a message on PC, or touch and hold on Mobile, to open a premium floating menu containing options to reply, copy text, heart, or react with emojis.
* **Interactive Micro-Reactions**: Hover over any chat bubble to access the quick emoji-reaction bar (👍, 🔥, 😂, ❤️, 😮, 👏).
* **Double-Tap to Heart**: Double-tap any message bubble on Mobile or double-click on PC to instantly leave a heart reaction.
* **Single Reaction Rule**: To prevent emoji spam, each user can only have one active emoji reaction on a single message at any time!
* **Typing Indicators**: Live visual alerts showing when your partner is drafting a response.

### 3. 💳 Digital Club Passport, vCard & QR Verification
Your digital identity as a member of the club is verified and accessible on demand:
* **Verified Student Badges**: Verified profiles are distinguished by verification checkmarks and custom badge symbols.
* **Printable ID Passport**: Access a visually stunning, printable, and downloadable digital club passport showing your official student registration, name, avatar, and XP Level.
* **Scan-to-Chat QR Integration**: Generates a standard URL QR code. Scanning or navigating to another student's QR path instantly opens up a verified direct message conversation with them!
* **vCard Contact QR Integration**: Generate a fully-codified contact file inside your QR. Smartphone cameras scanning this QR can instantly add you as a phone contact, importing your name, email, level, role, and skills dynamically!

### 4. 📈 Dynamic XP & Leveling Engine
AdvocoDe gamifies engagement! Participate in club activities to earn Experience Points (XP) and rank up from a **Code Cadet** to a **Master Architect**:
* **XP Leaderboard**: Track where you rank against other club members in the global rankings.
* **XP Progression & Achievements Hub**: View your active level progression, remaining XP until level-up, and unlock custom achievements!
* **Achievements Tracker**: Track personal challenges like "First Post", "Active Contributor", "Streak Master", and "Verification Badge" to boost your profile presence.
* **Active Status Badges**: Unlock custom ranking titles as your XP increases.
* **Real-Time Rewards**: Gain points instantly for posting in community chat (+5 XP), sending a message (+5 XP), liking a message (+5 XP), and interacting with discussions.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step User Guide (How to Use)

### Step 1: Set Up & Customize Your Student Profile
1. Navigate to the **Profile** section from the sidebar navigation menu.
2. Fill out or update your profile details:
   * Enter your **Display Name**.
   * Specify your official **Student Registration Number** (e.g. `CT102/G/1149/22`) to unlock your verified club status.
   * Upload a custom avatar or choose from pre-installed student avatars.
   * Write an eye-catching bio summarizing your favorite tech stacks (e.g., *React, Python, Tailwind, Machine Learning*).
3. Save your changes. Your digital club passport and achievements board will update instantly!

### Step 2: Post a Discussion or Code snippet
1. Navigate to the **Timeline Feed**.
2. Click inside the **"Share something with the club..."** compose box.
3. Select a category for your post (e.g., **Question** if you have a programming bug, or **Code Share** if you want to showcase a cool function).
4. (Optional) Toggle **Code Form** to include a clean code block. Paste your code and specify the language (e.g., `javascript`, `python`, `typescript`, `cpp`).
5. (Optional) Drag & drop or click to attach an illustrative image.
6. Click **Publish** to share it with the club. You will instantly gain **XP**!

### Step 3: Start a Direct Message (DM) Conversation & Use Gestures
1. Select the **Messenger / Chat** tab from the navigation sidebar.
2. If you don’t have any active chats yet, click the **`+` (Plus)** button next to the "Conversations" header.
3. Choose a club classmate from the alphabetical member directory list.
4. An elegant chat room will open. Type your message in the chat input and hit Send.
5. **Using Gesture Shortcuts**:
   * **Reply to a message**: On Mobile, swipe right on any chat bubble. On PC, hover and click the Reply icon, or click and hold the bubble for options.
   * **Reacting quickly**: Hover over a message to select an emoji, or double-tap/double-click the bubble to send a heart reaction!
   * **Message options menu**: Left-click and hold any bubble for 0.5 seconds on PC, or tap and hold on Mobile, to copy text, reply, or heart.

### Step 4: Scan and Verify a Classmate's Passport & Save Contacts
1. If a club classmate shares their passport image or scan QR link, use your mobile camera or follow the QR path.
2. Navigating to the passport link automatically loads their **Verified Digital Badge** on your screen.
3. Click the **vCard Contact** tab on the verification badge to render their codified contact details. Scanning this QR code with any smartphone camera instantly saves them to your phone's address book with all their profile metadata!
4. Click **"Message"** directly from the scanned badge popup to automatically launch a direct message conversation in real-time with no manual search required!

### Step 5: Toggling Light and Dark Mode
AdvocoDe features full light and dark mode styling. If you prefer working in high-contrast dark environments during late-night coding sprints:
* Click the theme switch toggle button (usually located in your sidebar or header settings).
* The entire platform, including all discussion boards, profile passports, sidebars, and the **entire Chat Messenger interface**, will transition smoothly into a stunning, eye-strain-free dark palette!
